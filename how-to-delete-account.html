How to Delete Your Account

If you would like to delete your ExitVPN account and remove your associated data, please contact our support team by email.

Request account deletion

Send an email to: support@exitvpn.com
 
To help us process your request faster, please include:
  • The email address associated with your ExitVPN account
  • A short message such as: “Please delete my ExitVPN account and all associated data.”

What happens next

After we receive your request, we will verify that you are the account owner and proceed with deleting your account and associated data.
 
If you have any questions, please reach out to support@exitvpn.com.
